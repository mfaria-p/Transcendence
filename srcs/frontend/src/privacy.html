42 Porto Project — Transcendence

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Transcendence platform collects, uses, and protects your data. This project is academic and intended for evaluation within the 42 Porto curriculum.

1. Data Collection

  • We collect basic account information (username, email) for authentication purposes.
  • Game data (matches, scores, statistics) is stored to provide platform functionality.
  • Session data and authentication tokens are used to maintain secure access.
  • Profile information (avatar, display name) is collected if provided by users.

2. Use of Data

  • Data is used exclusively to operate and demonstrate the Transcendence platform.
  • Information is processed to provide game features, user profiles, and matchmaking.
  • No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for commercial purposes.
  • Data may be used for academic evaluation and demonstration purposes within 42 Porto.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored in secure databases with access restricted to project team members.
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard encryption algorithms.
  • Authentication tokens are managed securely and expire after inactivity.
  • All data may be removed at the end of the academic evaluation period.

4. Third-Party Authentication

  • OAuth authentication (e.g., Google) may be used for account access.
  • When using third-party authentication, their respective privacy policies also apply.
  • We only receive necessary information (email, name) from OAuth providers.

5. Cookies and Tracking

  • Session cookies are used to maintain authentication state.
  • No third-party tracking or analytics tools are used for commercial purposes.
  • Cookies are essential for platform functionality and cannot be disabled while using the service.

6. User Rights

  • You can request access to your personal data stored in the platform.
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Data correction requests can be submitted to the project team.
  • Given the academic nature of the project, data retention policies may vary.

7. Data Retention

  • Data is retained for the duration of the academic project evaluation period.
  • All data may be permanently deleted after the project concludes.
  • Users will be notified if significant changes to data retention policies occur.

8. Children's Privacy

  • This platform is intended for use by 42 Porto students (18+ years old).
  • We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18 years of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

  • This Privacy Policy may be updated as the project evolves.
  • Significant changes will be communicated to active users.
  • Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of policy updates.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, data requests, or concerns, contact the Transcendence project team at 42 Porto.
